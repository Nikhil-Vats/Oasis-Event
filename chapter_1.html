Chapter 1 - Bit about BITS!

In this chapter, you have to answer 5 simple questions based on counter culture in our very own campus, you will given points on the basis of most selected answer.

If you select the MOST SELECTED ANSWER by previous players, you will get 50 points, in any other case you will get 20 points.

Chapter 2 - Shoot the bitches!

In this chapter you have to shoot the birds in the given time but the key is accuracy, you get 50 points for shooting a bird and 25 are deducted for an unsuccessful shoot(touch/click).
